Teaching

Throughout most of my maths career I've done teaching in some shape or form:

University of Copenhagen

  • I was one of the two lecturers for Algebraic Topology 1.5, two years in a row.
  • Mikkel Emil Thor Frøland wrote his Bachelor thesis entitled ``Dualizable Algebras'' under my supervision.
  • I supervised several reading courses on Topological Quantum Field Theory and related topics.

University of Oxford

The department also awarded me the Departmental Teaching Award in 2016.

  • University College:

    I was a Junior Lecturer for two years at my college, Univ. As part of this, I taught Prelims (first year's) Linear Algebra and Groups and Group Actions.

  • Williams-Exeter Programme:

    I ran one-on-one tutorials for an exchange student on Geometry of Surfaces.

  • Mathematical Institute:

    For the Oxford maths department, I've acted as

    • Class Tutor for Algebraic Topology, Homological Algebra, Lie Groups, and Computational Algebraic Topology.
    • Teaching assistant for Algebraic Topology, Homological Algebra, Lie Groups, Computational Algebraic Topology, Geometry of Surfaces, Topology and Groups.

  • Lincoln College:

I am a Stipendiary Lecturer in Algebra, tutoring for the first and second year algebra courses.

Utrecht University

During my time at Utrecht University, I was a student assistant for several courses: Multidimensional Real Analysis, Functional Analysis, Smooth Manifolds, and Riemann Surfaces.
